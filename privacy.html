Privacy Policy for Snitch

Effective Date: May 19, 2026

1. Introduction

This policy explains what information the Snitch Discord bot ("Snitch", "the bot", "we", "us") collects, why it is collected, how long it is kept, and the choices available to you. By using the bot, or by remaining in a voice or text channel where the bot is active, you acknowledge this policy. Questions may be directed to the contact in Section 11.

2. Information We Collect

The bot processes the following categories of data:

  • User identifiers — Discord user IDs and display names of speakers present during a recording.
  • Guild and channel metadata — server IDs, channel IDs, role IDs, and message IDs needed to operate commands.
  • Command arguments — text supplied when a command is run.
  • Voice data — when an authorised member starts a recording, the bot captures the audio of every speaker in the channel and generates a speech-to-text transcript of that audio.
  • Server configuration — per-server settings such as command prefix, filter words, role rules, and channel blacklists.

The bot does not collect message content beyond what is required to parse its own commands, does not collect IP addresses or payment information, and uses no advertising or web tracking.

3. How We Use Information

Collected data is used only to operate the service, specifically to:

  • Execute bot commands and return responses.
  • Produce and deliver voice recordings and transcripts requested by authorised members.
  • Detect configured filter words and alert the roles a server has designated.
  • Persist per-server settings between sessions.
  • Diagnose errors and maintain the service.

4. Data Storage and Retention

  • Recordings and transcripts are stored on private hardware controlled by the bot operator, on storage protected by full-disk encryption. They are retained until deleted by the operator or upon request (see Section 6).
  • Shareable links are generated by uploading copies of files to third-party file hosts (see Section 5). Temporary audio and live-transcript links expire automatically after 72 hours. Polished-transcript links may remain available until deleted by the operator.
  • Server configuration is retained for as long as the bot remains in the server and may be cleared after it leaves.
  • Diagnostic logs record connection events and errors and are retained on a best-effort basis.

We cannot retroactively delete links or files that other people have already downloaded or copied.

We do not sell, trade, or rent personal data. We may disclose information where required by law or valid legal process.

6. Your Rights

You may contact the bot operator (see Section 11) to request:

  • Access — a summary of the data the bot holds about you.
  • Deletion — removal of your recordings, transcripts, and any configuration entries keyed to your user ID.
  • Correction — removal of an inaccurate transcript line.
  • Withdrawal of consent — for any future processing of your data.

Verified requests are processed within a reasonable period. Temporary third-party links cannot be force-deleted and will expire on their own schedule. If a server has mirrored a recording elsewhere, that copy is controlled by that server's administrators.

7. Recording Consent

Recording voice communications is privacy-sensitive and is regulated differently across jurisdictions. The bot has no technical means of excluding an individual speaker from a recording. If you do not consent to being recorded, leave the voice channel before recording begins, or ask a server administrator to add the channel to the recording blacklist.

8. Security

We apply reasonable security measures appropriate to a free, independently operated bot, including full-disk encryption of stored files and restricted access to credentials. No system is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee data against all threats. If you have heightened privacy needs, do not speak in voice channels where the bot is recording.

9. Children's Privacy

The bot is not intended for anyone under 13, or the higher minimum age required by Discord in your region. If we become aware that data has been collected from a child below the applicable age, we will delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected in the Effective Date above. We will not retroactively apply more invasive processing to data already collected without notice.
