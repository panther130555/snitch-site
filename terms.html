2. Description of Service

Snitch is a Discord bot that, when an authorised member runs the appropriate command:

  1. Joins a voice channel and records the audio of every speaker present.
  2. Transcribes that audio into text.
  3. Generates shareable links to the recording and transcript through third-party file hosts.
  4. Posts those links into Discord, optionally mirroring them to a configured backup channel.
  5. Detects configured "filter words" and alerts a designated role.
  6. Stores per-server configuration such as prefixes, filter words, role rules, and blacklists.

A full feature list is available through the help command.
