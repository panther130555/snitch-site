What the bot does

  • Joins voice channels and records per-user audio (WAV) and a live transcript
  • Transcribes speech locally with Whisper (offline)
  • Pings a configured role when a filter word is spoken, with a 10-second audio clip
  • Uploads recordings and transcripts to catbox.moe / litterbox.catbox.moe for shareable links
  • Per-server prefix, filter list, VC auto-roles, recording blacklist, backup channel
  • Server administrators control configuration; the operator controls global access
